About

We offer practices in Yoga Asana, Pranayama, Meditation, and Yoga Nidra, daly classes ranging for beginners to the most advanced students. Come in anytime.

We are also conducting yoga and meditation classes for students in few schools in our area.

Our instructor is M.P Vijayan who holds a Diploma in Yogic Sciences and have 20+ years of experience in practising and training in yoga and meditation.

We also provide diet plans to those who want to loss weight and for those who suffer from gastric problems.
